One of my earliest apps, built and released on Google Play nearly a decade ago. Input what ingredients you have, get matching recipes with both text instructions and YouTube video tutorials.

Backend on Firebase for storage and real-time sync. Built in native Android (Java and XML), optimized to run smoothly on the mid-range devices most people had at the time. My first public-facing app.
