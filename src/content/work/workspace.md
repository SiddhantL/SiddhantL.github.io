Started as a freelance project and turned into my personal productivity hub. It packed project tracking with taskboards, a media hosting gallery, a blog editor with publishing, secure notes, and a dynamic resume builder into one app.

Built with Flutter for cross-platform and Firebase for real-time sync. The goal was something as cohesive as Notion but custom-fit to how I actually work, not how a PM at a SaaS company thinks I should.
