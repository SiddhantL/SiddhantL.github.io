To add a new blog post, create a `.md` file in `src/content/blog/` with this frontmatter:

```yaml
---
title: Your Title
description: A short summary.
publishDate: 2026-05-23
tags:
  - Tag1
  - Tag2
---
```

Then just write markdown below it. Push and it's live.
